Why Search with Us?

Always Free
There is never a charge for this service. We provide free race listings for your local community.
What Are We About?
We are your local race calendar. OurNextRace.com was founded in 2018 to make it easier for people to find their perfect race.
Who Can Post Races?
All runners and race directors can add races to their Race Calendar for free. They can be running, track, bicycling, hiking, ... any events are welcome. Once approved they will appear for the general public. Elementary, high school and boys and girls team events can also be included. All active outdoor events.
Can I Customize the List?
Yes, that is the ultimate goal! Not only can you search for races in your local area, you can also add races to your private calendar and track them.

Our mission is to help you discover active experiences

OurNextRace.com was founded in 2018 to make it easier for you to find a perfect race for you and your friends. The website started as a technical showcase but instead has become a free service for the community.
