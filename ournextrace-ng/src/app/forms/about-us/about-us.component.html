Who We Are

OurNextRace.com was created to answer a simple question quickly and share with your friends. When is our next race? Usually this questions comes up after your last race, when you see someone new or during a training run and bump into friends.

This web site started as a vehicle to learn the latest technologies and keep the saw sharp. After a while rather than just say you know about Angular, REST, Java, Spring, AWS, Cloud Programming, RDMS, HTML, CSS and the endless alphabet soup of technologies why not build a website that will benefit from all of this work. Also since you love sharing why not make the site free to avoid dealing with pay walls, subscriptions. If you do something you love, it's not work. And if you can share it with your friends it makes the hours and costs all worth it.

While there is no guarantee of perfection there is a 100% assurance that we'll take your feedback seriously. Let us know and above find your next race with us. Be epic!
